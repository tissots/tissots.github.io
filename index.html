About

Who I am

I'm Brian — an embedded systems engineer based in Nairobi, Kenya, with a diploma in electrical & electronics engineering. I specialise in IoT product design: from the first schematic all the way to fabricated PCBs and shipped firmware.

I'm currently on industrial attachment at Kenya Power and Lighting Company (KPLC), where I'm developing a transformer fault monitoring concept that could scale across East Africa's power grid.

On the side, I run a YouTube channel teaching electronics and embedded systems to Kenyan and African makers — the kind of content I wish I had when I was starting out.

Services I offer

PCB design in KiCad (schematic to Gerbers), ESP32 & STM32 firmware development, IoT product prototyping, and electronics consulting. Available on Fiverr and Upwork.

Currently building

HomeNode smart home system, transformer monitoring device for KPLC, and NyumbaEasy — a rental listing PWA for Kenyan landlords and tenants.
